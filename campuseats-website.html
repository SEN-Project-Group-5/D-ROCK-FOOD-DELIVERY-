About CampusEats

A delivery network built for — and by — NBU students

CampusEats started as a simple observation: NBU students were already ordering food across hostels and faculties through scattered WhatsApp chats and word-of-mouth runners. There was no verification, no order tracking, and no guarantee your food — or your money — would arrive.

We built a closed-loop platform instead. Every customer, vendor, and courier on CampusEats is a verified member of the NBU community. Orders are tracked from the kitchen to your exact hostel block, and every delivery is confirmed with a one-time code so nothing goes missing in the last few metres.

Our mission is simple: make it fast, fair, and safe for students to eat well without leaving their studies — and give student couriers a reliable way to earn on campus.

What "closed-loop" means

Nobody outside NBU can order, deliver, or sell on CampusEats. Every account is verified against an NBU email or student ID before it goes live.

What "last-meter" means

We don't deliver to "campus" in general — we deliver to Andy Hall Block 3, not just Andy Hall. Precision is the whole point.

Terms and Conditions

These terms govern use of CampusEats, a closed-loop food ordering and delivery platform available only to verified members of the Nigerian British University community.

1. Eligibility

Access is limited to individuals with a valid NBU email address or verified NBU student ID. CampusEats reserves the right to suspend accounts that cannot be verified or that are used outside their intended role.

2. Orders and payment

Customers agree to pay the listed price for menu items plus the applicable service fee at checkout. Vendors are responsible for the accuracy of their menu listings and pricing.

3. Courier conduct

Couriers must deliver orders to the correct zone and node, and confirm delivery using the customer's one-time code. Misuse of the courier role may result in suspension.

4. Platform role

CampusEats facilitates the connection between customers, vendors, and couriers. It does not prepare food and is not liable for food quality, except as set out in the Refund Policy.

5. Changes to these terms

CampusEats may update these terms from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

Privacy Policy

CampusEats collects only the information needed to operate a closed-loop delivery service for NBU: your NBU email, student ID (where applicable), delivery zone, order history, and — for couriers and vendors — payout details.

What we collect

  • Account details: name, NBU email, role (student, vendor, courier)
  • Order data: items ordered, delivery zone, order status and timestamps
  • Verification data: NBU email domain or student ID used for account approval

How we use it

Data is used to route orders to the correct vendor and courier, verify eligibility, and generate the sales and earnings reports shown in vendor and courier dashboards. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Data retention

Order and account data is retained only as long as needed to operate the platform and resolve disputes, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

Legal

Refund Policy

CampusEats wants every order delivered correctly and on time. If something goes wrong, here's how refunds are handled.

Eligible for a refund

  • Order marked Delivered but never received at your zone
  • Wrong items delivered, or items missing from your order
  • Order cancelled by the vendor after payment

Not eligible for a refund

  • Change of mind after the vendor has marked the order Preparing
  • Incorrect delivery zone entered by the customer at checkout

How to request one

Contact support with your order ID within 24 hours of delivery. Approved refunds are credited back to your original payment method within 3–5 business days.
